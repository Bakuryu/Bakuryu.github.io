Nowhere

Elise awakes to find herself in the unknown world of Nowhere. With no idea how she got there or where she is she must utilize stealth and cunning to make her way through the world full of monstrous creatures and elaborate traps. Weapons are scarce and to stand and fight could be suicide. She must find a way to escape this hellish place.

Treasure Hutner

Jack is a pirate who loves his treasure and will go to great lengths to get it. Your job is to help Jack find three treasure chests while avoiding death, literally!

Pixelnoid

An Arkanoid clone where each level and power up is themed from NES 8-bit games such as Mega Man, Super Mario Brothers, and Legend of Zelda.

Zombie Survival (Working Title)

Survive in a maze of zombies for as long as you can in this Pacman styled game with a twist. Build turrets to better your odds at survival as the number of zombies increases each round. Use different turrets for strategies to maximize your score and survival time..
