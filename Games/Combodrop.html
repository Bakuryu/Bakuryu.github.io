Overview

Combo Drop is a game developed by Cody Casey, David Machov, and myself at the Philly Game Jam 2016. Our themes, which were everyone choose randomly, were: Just out of reach, diversity in combination, and minimalism. The result was Combo drop a colorful, yet simple shape stacking game that requires you to quickly reach the top as the floor sinks below you.

Since the game jame I have been porting the game to Unity as the original was done in javascript, which I was not as familiar with. Along with porting the game, I am also implementing the two additional power ups and additional features we never go to add during the game jam.

Contributions

The development of this game was an interesting experience as it was done outside my comfort zone in terms of development experience. Our other coder, Cody, took point on the coding side as he was much more familiar with javascript than I was. That said I did what ever I could and learn a lot along the way
