Overview

Nowhere is a survival horror game that focuses on avoiding enemies rather than confronting them. Players play as Elise who wakes up in the unknown world of Nowhere. With limited weaponry players must avoid enemies whenever possibly and can use the environment such as a dumpster to hide when enemies become to close. Players must maneuver through the environment of Nowhere to discover its secret and figure out how to escape it.

Contributions

My roles for this project were both as a project manager and programmer. I organized the tasks we needed to accomplish, received estimations of completion times from teammates, and tracked our progress all using Microsoft Project.
