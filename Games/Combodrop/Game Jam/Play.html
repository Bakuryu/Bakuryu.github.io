How to Play

The objective of the game is to stack the shapes up to the dotted line. To move a shape into position simply move your mouse left or right. To drop a shape left click the mouse. Stack carefully and quickly because the floor is sinking!

**Warning** Keep your mouse in between the lighter area in order for you mouse clicks to register, if you start to see your mouse cursor you've begun to move to far out!

Powerup

There is only one power up implemented in this version of the game. That power up is the Grow powerup shown below.

This power up will comes in the same three colors the shapes do is activated when it touches a shape of the same color it is. When activated it englarges the shape it made contact with. Beware though that if it makes contact with a shape that does not match its color there is a chance it will destroy it.
