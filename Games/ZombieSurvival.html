Overview

Zombie Survival is a top down maze game where the player must survival waves and waves of zombies using only turrets, traps, and gadget purchased or earned by surviving previous waves. Players must strategize their placement of turrets and traps to maximize their effectiveness for survival and points to spend for the next wave.
